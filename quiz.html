International Women's Day (IWD) is a global celebration of the achievements, resilience, and contributions of women throughout history.
It serves as a powerful reminder of the ongoing fight for gender equality, women's rights, and social progress. The theme for IWD 2025, 'Accelerate Action,' for a united effort to drive meaningful change in various sectors, from education and technology to leadership and sustainability. By recognizing the remarkable women who have paved the way and empowering future generations, we can create a world where equality is not just an idea but a reality.
